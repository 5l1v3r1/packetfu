PacketFu

A library for reading a writing packets to an interface or to a libpcap-formatted file. It is maintained at code.google.com/p/packetfu/

Installation

PacketFu should live somewhere in your path. I haven‘t decided yet on packaging, mainly because I need to figure out what works best for Linux/Win/OSX (and FreeBSD, and Solaris maybe); your best bet is to just drop it into /usr/local/lib/site_ruby/1.8 or where ever you install bindata to.

Requirements

BinData: bindata.rubyforge.org

PcapRub: www.metasploit.com/svn/framework3/trunk/external/pcaprub
